Agentic AI

Rehab Agent

Garmin, N8N and Claude automated personalised weekly training plans and data visualization. Combines objective metrics from your Garmin data, with subjective feedback from your form input, and generares a weekly email with your upcoming weeks training plan.

PWA / AI

NoSh

Upload meal photos, get personalized nutrition goals. Gemini 2.0 Flash analyzes eating patterns and generates actionable recommendations. Built as Next.js 15 PWA with Supabase edge functions.

Full Product

My Maths Trainer

I helped design, build and launch, with a maths teacher friend, an interactive web platform for Junior and Leaving Cert students in Ireland to learn Maths. It has gamified lessons, personalised learning paths and aims to drive higher marks in the Irish Junior and Leaving Cert. This has full backend, frontend and security measures in place.

AI Prototyping

Planning Advisor

For a Hackathon, I wanted to create an end to end flow for performance coaches to create detailed, periodised training plans. Performance coaches at elite sports teams face a problem creating detailed, periodised training plans due to time pressures. I wanted to automate that using AI.

Chrome Extension

SlugTranslate

Inline English-Spanish translation without leaving your tab. Select text, press Alt+A, and translation appears in bottom-right overlay. Built with Chrome Manifest V3, Preact, and MyMemory API.

iOS App
