Projects & Thoughts

iOS App

Intelligram for Instagram

An iOS app leveraging machine learning to analyze Instagram photos and predict engagement based on image quality patterns. This project reached 50,000+ users and analyzed 3+ million photos, but taught me that technical success doesn't overcome founder misalignment.

Full Product

My Maths Trainer

I helped design, build and launch, with a maths teacher friend, an interactive web platform for Junior and Leaving Cert students in Ireland to learn Maths. It has gamified lessons, personalised learning paths and aims to drive higher marks in the Irish Junior and Leaving Cert. This has full backend, frontend and security measures in place.

Strategy

Design Moats

Design moats are things that reinforce the design teams position and competitive advantage. They are things that create value for the business and drive its competitive advantage. These may be larger or smaller in size and impact, they may be internal production processes or external value adds.

Agentic AI

Agent Based Rehab Plan

After Achilles surgery, I needed to track my rehab carefully. My Garmin watch was collecting loads of data, but it was stuck in the Garmin Connect app. I built an agentic system that runs automatically, understands my situation, and gives me useful recommendations.
